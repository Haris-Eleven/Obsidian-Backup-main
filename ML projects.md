3. 5. **AI-Powered Resume Screening and Matching**  
— **Objective**: Build a model to automatically screen and match resumes to job descriptions, reducing the manual effort for recruiters.  
— **Challenges**:  
— Parsing varied resume formats and understanding unstructured text.  
— Ensuring fair, unbiased, and non-discriminatory predictions.  
— Matching skills and experiences effectively to job requirements.  
— **Techniques & Tools:**  
— NLP (to extract entities like skills, qualifications), semantic similarity models, deep learning, Elasticsearch for fast searches.  
— **Extensions**: Add a feedback loop for recruiters to improve model accuracy, or create a candidate ranking system based on job relevance.
